Cambodia Itinerary: A Perfect 10-Day Journey

[Main Cover Image: Cambodia Travel Landscape]

Cambodia is a captivating country steeped in history, culture, and natural beauty. From the awe-inspiring temples of Angkor to serene beaches and vibrant cities, this 10-day Cambodia itinerary gives you a balanced mix of adventure, heritage, and relaxation.

Day 1: Arrival in Siem Reap

[Image: Siem Reap Night Market or Pub Street]

Fly into Siem Reap, the gateway to Angkor. Spend the day settling in and exploring the night markets or relaxing with a Khmer massage.

Highlights:
  • Pub Street nightlife
  • Artisans Angkor
  • Try local delicacies like Amok curry

Day 2–3: Discover Angkor Wat & Temples

[Image: Angkor Wat at Sunrise]

Dedicate two days to exploring the ancient wonders of Angkor Archaeological Park.

Must-See Temples:
  • Angkor Wat (sunrise visit is a must)
  • Bayon Temple (with its iconic smiling faces)
  • Ta Prohm (Tomb Raider temple)
  • Banteay Srei (intricate carvings)

Tip: Hire a local guide for historical context and hidden spots.

Day 4: Floating Villages & Countryside

[Image: Floating Village on Tonlé Sap Lake]

Visit the floating village of Kampong Phluk or Chong Kneas on Tonlé Sap Lake. Experience rural Cambodian life.

Optional: Bicycle through rice paddies or visit a local silk farm.

Day 5: Travel to Phnom Penh

[Image: Phnom Penh Riverfront or Bus Travel]

Take a morning bus or flight to Phnom Penh (approx. 6 hours by road). Enjoy an evening stroll along the Mekong River promenade.

Evening Activity: Sunset cruise on the Tonlé Sap and Mekong rivers.

Day 6: Phnom Penh – History & Culture

[Image: Royal Palace or Tuol Sleng Genocide Museum]

Explore Cambodia’s complex history and vibrant capital.

Must-Visit:
  • Royal Palace & Silver Pagoda
  • Tuol Sleng Genocide Museum
  • Choeung Ek Killing Fields

Evening: Dine by the riverside or try a cooking class.

Day 7: Head to Kampot

[Image: Kampot Riverside or Pepper Farms]

Travel to Kampot, a laid-back riverside town known for its colonial charm and pepper plantations.

Highlights:
  • Kampot Pepper Farms
  • Sunset kayak on the Praek Tuek Chhu River
  • Explore Bokor Hill Station

Day 8: Day Trip to Kep

[Image: Kep Crab Market or Rabbit Island]

Take a short ride to Kep for a day of seafood and sea views.

Things to Do:
  • Crab Market
  • Kep National Park Hike
  • Relax on the quiet beach or take a boat to Rabbit Island

Day 9: Back to Phnom Penh

[Image: Phnom Penh Rooftop or Market Shopping]

Return to Phnom Penh for your final night. Explore any missed attractions or shop at the Russian Market.

Dinner Idea: Try a rooftop restaurant for great views of the city.

Day 10: Departure

[Image: Souvenir Shop or Airport]

Depending on your flight, enjoy a leisurely breakfast or last-minute souvenir shopping before heading to the airport.

Travel Tips:

  • Currency: Cambodian Riel (KHR), but USD is widely accepted
  • Best Time to Visit: November to March (dry season)
  • Transport: Tuk-tuks, buses, or private transfers are common
  • Visa: Most nationalities can get a visa on arrival or online

With this 10-day Cambodia itinerary, you’ll immerse yourself in the rich culture, tragic history, and natural splendor of one of Southeast Asia’s most underrated gems.
