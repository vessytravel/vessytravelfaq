Travel Planning Process

Our travel planning process is designed to be thorough yet straightforward:

1. Consultation: We begin with an in-depth conversation about your travel dreams, preferences, budget, and any special requirements. This helps us understand exactly what you're looking for.

2. Customization: Based on our consultation, we research and design a personalized itinerary tailored specifically to your needs. This includes accommodations, activities, transportation, and special experiences.

3. Refinement: We present the proposed itinerary to you and make any adjustments needed until it perfectly matches your vision.

4. Booking: Once you approve the final plan, we handle all the reservations and logistics, ensuring everything is confirmed and organized.

5. Support: Throughout your journey, we provide ongoing support and assistance, addressing any questions or concerns that may arise.

For the best experience and most options, we recommend starting your travel planning process:

• 9-12 months ahead for peak-season travel, special occasions like honeymoons, or trips to destinations with limited accommodations

• 6-9 months ahead for most international journeys

• 3-6 months ahead for less complex domestic trips

Planning well in advance allows us to secure the best accommodations, expert guides, and exclusive experiences that might otherwise be unavailable. It also gives us time to carefully craft every detail of your journey.

That said, we understand that sometimes travel opportunities arise unexpectedly. We're always happy to work with shorter timeframes when necessary and will do our utmost to create an exceptional experience regardless of the planning window.

To begin crafting your perfect journey, we'll need some essential information:

• Destination interests or specific locations you wish to visit

• Approximate travel dates and duration

• Number of travelers and their ages (if traveling with children)

• Accommodation preferences (luxury hotels, boutique properties, villas, etc.)

• Special interests or activities you'd like to experience

• Previous travel experiences you've particularly enjoyed

• Budget guidelines to help us plan appropriately

• Any mobility considerations or special requirements

The more details you can provide, the better we can tailor our recommendations to match your preferences. Don't worry if you're unsure about some aspects—part of our job is helping you explore the possibilities!

Pricing & Booking

Our pricing structure is designed to be transparent while reflecting the value of our personalized service:

• We typically charge a planning fee that covers the extensive research, design, and coordination of your custom itinerary. This fee varies based on the complexity of the trip and number of travelers.

• The total trip cost includes all accommodations, activities, specified meals, transfers, and other elements outlined in your final itinerary.

• We provide a detailed breakdown of costs so you understand exactly what's included in your package.

• Our relationships with luxury properties and experience providers often allow us to secure added amenities and perks not available to the general public, enhancing the value of your investment.

We work with a range of budgets and are always upfront about costs. Our goal is to create extraordinary experiences that represent excellent value for the investment, focusing on quality rather than simply finding the lowest price.

Our standard payment and cancellation policies are as follows, though they may vary slightly depending on specific supplier requirements:

Payment Schedule:

• Planning fee: Due at the time of engagement

• Initial deposit: 25-30% of the total trip cost due upon approval of the itinerary

• Final payment: Typically due 60-90 days before departure, depending on the destination

Cancellation Policy:

• Planning fees are non-refundable as they cover work already performed

• 120+ days before departure: Full refund of deposit (minus any non-refundable elements)

• 90-119 days: 75% refund of deposit

• 60-89 days: 50% refund of deposit

• Under 60 days: Non-refundable

We strongly recommend purchasing comprehensive travel insurance to protect your investment. We can advise on appropriate insurance options for your specific journey.

While we don't directly sell travel insurance, we strongly recommend it for all travelers and can connect you with reputable providers who offer comprehensive coverage.

Quality travel insurance typically covers:

• Trip cancellation and interruption

• Emergency medical expenses and evacuation

• Lost or delayed baggage

• Travel delays

• Certain COVID-19 related disruptions (policy dependent)

We can help you assess your specific needs and recommend appropriate coverage levels based on your destination, activities, and other factors. This allows you to make an informed decision and secure the protection that's right for your journey.

In today's unpredictable travel environment, insurance is an essential component of responsible travel planning, particularly for premium experiences with significant financial investment.

Required travel documents vary by destination, but we provide detailed guidance throughout the planning process. Generally, you should be prepared with:

• Passport: Valid for at least 6 months beyond your return date with sufficient blank pages

• Visas: Required for many destinations; we'll advise on specific requirements and application procedures

• Travel Insurance: Documentation of your policy

• Vaccination Records: Including any destination-specific requirements

• Driver's License: If you plan to drive (and possibly an International Driving Permit)

• Travel Itinerary: Comprehensive documentation we provide with confirmations and contact information

We'll provide a detailed checklist specific to your journey and can answer any questions about documentation requirements. For certain destinations, we can also arrange expedited visa services through our trusted partners.
