Cambodia Itinerary: A Perfect 10-Day Journey

 

Cambodia is a captivating country steeped in history, culture, and natural beauty. From the awe-inspiring temples of Angkor to serene beaches and vibrant cities, this 10-day Cambodia itinerary gives you a balanced mix of adventure, heritage, and relaxation.

Day 1: Arrival in Siem Reap

Fly into Siem Reap, the gateway to Angkor. Spend the day settling in and exploring the night markets or relaxing with a Khmer massage.

  • Pub Street nightlife
  • Artisans Angkor
  • Try local delicacies like Amok curry

Day 2–3: Discover Angkor Wat & Temples

Dedicate two days to exploring the ancient wonders of Angkor Archaeological Park.

  • Angkor Wat (sunrise visit is a must)
  • Bayon Temple
  • Ta Prohm (Tomb Raider temple)
  • Banteay Srei

Day 4: Floating Villages & Countryside

Visit the floating village of Kampong Phluk or Chong Kneas on Tonlé Sap Lake. Experience rural Cambodian life. Optional: Bicycle through rice paddies or visit a local silk farm.

Day 5: Travel to Phnom Penh

Take a morning bus or flight to Phnom Penh. Enjoy a stroll along the Mekong River promenade. Consider a sunset cruise.

Day 6: Phnom Penh – History & Culture

  • Royal Palace & Silver Pagoda
  • Tuol Sleng Genocide Museum
  • Choeung Ek Killing Fields

In the evening, dine by the riverside or join a Cambodian cooking class.

Day 7: Head to Kampot

  • Kampot Pepper Farms
  • Kayak on the Praek Tuek Chhu River
  • Bokor Hill Station

Day 8: Day Trip to Kep

  • Crab Market
  • Kep National Park
  • Rabbit Island boat trip

Day 9: Return to Phnom Penh

Shop at the Russian Market, visit a spa, or explore more of Phnom Penh’s vibrant food scene on your final full day.

Day 10: Departure

Depending on your flight time, enjoy a relaxing breakfast and head to the airport with unforgettable memories.

Travel Tips

  • Currency: Cambodian Riel (KHR), USD widely accepted
  • Best Time to Visit: November to March
  • Transport: Tuk-tuks, buses, private taxis
  • Visa: Available on arrival or online

With this 10-day Cambodia itinerary, you’ll experience the best of what this beautiful country has to offer—from the mystical Angkor temples to riverside towns and cultural insights that will stay with you for a lifetime.
